Food Delevery Website

I’m excited to showcase Foodzz, an end-to-end food ordering and delivery management platform I’ve been building, designed to provide a smooth and modern food-tech experience for all users. Customers can browse different restaurants, explore categories, and select from a wide variety of food items, add them to their cart, place orders, enter delivery addresses, make secure checkouts, and track their food in real time until it arrives. Restaurants have their own panel where they can add and manage food items, view and process incoming orders, and monitor overall performance. Delivery partners get a dedicated dashboard that allows them to accept orders, update their delivery status, and manage their delivery history. At the top of the system, the Admin Panel provides full operational control, with options to add and manage restaurants, register and monitor delivery partners, create and organize categories, manage promotional sliders, and oversee the entire order ecosystem. Foodzz is powered by a robust Django backend, a responsive TailwindCSS frontend, JavaScript for interactive features, and PostgreSQL as the database to ensure scalability and performance. This project has helped me combine design, functionality, and technology into one integrated solution that connects customers, restaurants, and delivery partners seamlessly.

Technologies

  • Backend: Python, Django Framework
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS
  • Database: PostgreSQL
